Features

Everything you need to explore human knowledge

From raw retrieval to AI-generated explanations - the entire pipeline, automated.

🔍

Intelligent Retrieval

Simultaneously searches Wikipedia, arXiv research papers, and Semantic Scholar to gather real, cited knowledge from authoritative sources.

🧠

AI-Powered Analysis

Artificial intelligence Model reads the retrieved sources and generates a structured multi-paragraph explanation covering origins, history, and significance.

⚡

Semantic Matching

SentenceTransformer embeddings and FAISS vector search detect when your idea already exists under a different name, preventing duplicates.

📦

Smart Caching

Inspired by LRU and LFU cache algorithms — frequently accessed concepts stay hot, rare ones move to archive, mirroring CPU memory hierarchy.

🏛️

Knowledge History

Every concept is saved with its canonical name and your original description, building a personal knowledge cache that grows with every search.

🔗

Source Linking

Every explanation is backed by direct links to original Wikipedia articles, arXiv papers, and Semantic Scholar publications for full traceability.

How It Works

From idea to insight in seconds

1

Submit Your Idea

Type any concept, invention, or discovery. The system understands intent regardless of phrasing.

2

AI Retrieves Sources

Wikipedia, arXiv, and Semantic Scholar are searched. Results are cached for instant future access.

3

Vector Search & Rerank

FAISS finds the most semantically similar content. A CrossEncoder reranks by relevance to your exact query.

4

LLM Explains

Large Language Model generates a rich, sourced explanation saved permanently to your knowledge cache.
